ECG Design

Cadence Design Systems, Altium

Sep - Oct 2024

This project involved designing and building an ECG instrumentation amplifier circuit. I developed the schematics using Cadence, and then transitioned to Altium Designer to create the PCB layout. The PCB includes careful placement of components, ground planes, and routing optimized for signal integrity. After finalizing the design, I collaborated with a manufacturer for production and performed assembly and soldering of components. This project highlights a complete design flow from concept to physical board implementation.
